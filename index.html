My favourite books

Martin Eden by Jack London can be classified as a: Künstlerroman: a novel that focuses on the artistic development and struggles of its protagonist, in this case, Martin Eden, a young writer. This genre is characterized by its exploration of the artist’s inner life, creative process, and emotional struggles.

The Theory of Everything: The Origin and Fate of the Universe” by Stephen Hawking is a book that attempts to explain the origin and fate of the universe, as well as the fundamental laws that govern it. The book is a collection of seven lectures delivered by Hawking over the years, covering topics such as:

  • 1 - The expanding universe and black holes.
  • 2 - The origin and fate of the universe.
  • 3 - The direction of time.
  • 4 - The theory of everything (a hypothetical, all-encompassing theory that would unify all physical phenomena).
